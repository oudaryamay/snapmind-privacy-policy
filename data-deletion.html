Data Deletion Policy

Effective Date: June 12, 2026

SnapMind follows a privacy-first design. The application does not upload or store user data on external servers.

Where Your Data Is Stored

  • Screenshot metadata is stored locally on your device.
  • OCR results are stored locally on your device.
  • Saved tags and notes remain on your device.
  • No user data is stored on developer-controlled servers.

How to Delete Your Data

Because all information remains on your device, you can permanently delete your data using either method below:

  • Uninstall SnapMind from your device.
  • Clear application data from Android Settings.

Server Data Requests

SnapMind does not maintain user accounts and does not store personal data on external servers. Therefore, there is no server-side user data available for deletion requests.
